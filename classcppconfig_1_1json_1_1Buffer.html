Detailed Description

A class to manage a buffer of characters efficiently.

The Buffer class provides mechanisms to navigate and manipulate a sequence of characters. It supports operations such as reading the next character, checking if the end of the data has been reached, matching a sequence of characters, extracting a substring, counting characters based on a condition, moving forward in the buffer, and retrieving the current position in terms of line and column.
